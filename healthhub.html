Challenge

Shifting perception from COVID testing to full-spectrum healthcare

When HealthHub launched just before the pandemic, it became synonymous with COVID testing. By 2023–24, however, the brand had grown into a nationwide chain of multi-specialty clinics and pharmacies. With 20+ centers across the UAE, HealthHub needed a strategic relaunch to reposition itself as a modern, patient-first primary care provider. The challenge was to build awareness of its expanded services, convey the blend of technology and compassion, and create a unifying brand message that resonated with patients, staff, and doctors alike, while achieving mass visibility across the UAE.

Approach

A patient-first relaunch grounded in listening and empathy

To reposition HealthHub, aló designed a campaign anchored in the insight that patients want to feel heard. Our approach combined human-centered storytelling, multi-channel amplification, and experiential engagement

1. Research & Insightss

We identified the perception gap: patients often feel overlooked. HealthHub’s technology and integrated care provide a solution that balances efficiency with empathy.

2. Creative Strategy

Developed the campaign idea “We Hear You”, positioning HealthHub as a brand that truly listens. Hero film featured doctors pledging to understand and treat patients with care.

3. Activation & Experiential Launch

Large-scale laser and water show to publicly relaunch the brand, creating press and social buzz. Influencer engagement prompted conversations on care, forming a live collage of the HealthHub logo from community photos.

4. OOH & Multi-Channel Rollout

Large-format awareness across key UAE locations emphasizing the “We Hear You” promise; integrated messaging across clinics, social, and digital channels.

Results

Repositioning achieved with scale and impact

The campaign successfully reintroduced HealthHub as a modern, technology-enabled, multi-specialty provider

3.9M unique reach

250K+ engagements

54K+ website visits

8M impressions across channels

Conclusion

Through “We Hear You”, aló helped HealthHub transform public perception and reinforce its position as a modern, patient-centric healthcare network. By combining human storytelling, strategic digital marketing, and high-impact activations, the campaign built awareness, trust, and engagement across audiences.
