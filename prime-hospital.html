Challenge

Consistently highlighting medical expertise across specializations

Prime Hospitals wanted to spotlight a different service each month, creating an annual calendar to showcase its diverse specialties. The challenge was to position the brand as a trusted expert while engaging both current and potential patients. Each campaign needed to be relevant, memorable, and relatable, especially when communicating complex medical topics. The brand sought a solution that could maintain creativity and consistency throughout the year, building awareness, trust, and patient engagement across multiple touchpoints.

Approach

Building a year-long platform to showcase expertise

To address the challenge, aló developed a creative and communication platform that could extend seamlessly across the year:

1. Strategic Insights & Planning

We mapped a monthly content calendar highlighting key specializations. Audience research ensured each spotlight resonated with patients and caregivers, creating relevance and anticipation.

2. Creative Platform & Storytelling

The #WeKnow[specialization] framework allowed consistent branding while giving each campaign its own personality. #WeKnowPaediatrics, inspired by children’s imagination, connected with parents through relatable, engaging storytelling.

3. Digital & Audience Engagement

The campaign ran across social media and targeted ads, paired with lead-generation forms. Interactive content amplified engagement and ensured the message reached the right audience.

4. Measurement & Optimization

Performance tracking guided iterative improvements. Insights on engagement and conversions ensured the campaign remained effective and impactful throughout the year.

Results

Driving engagement, leads, and long-term brand impact

The campaign delivered tangible results and strengthened Prime Hospitals’s position as a trusted healthcare provider:

Over 1,000 conversions from initial activations

Strong rise in leads via calls, generating 18x ROI

Year-long activity resulted in 14% growth in followers

2,200 leads captured through online forms

65% return users activated, showing ongoing audience engagement

Conclusion

aló helped Prime Hospitals transform its approach to specialization marketing by creating a flexible, year-long campaign platform. Through strategic insights, compelling storytelling, and targeted digital engagement, the brand successfully showcased its expertise, strengthened patient trust, and delivered measurable business impact.
