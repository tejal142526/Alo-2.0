Website & event branding

Movement Health Foundation

Movement Health Foundation, a Swiss based NGO working to revolutionise global healthcare access, wanted to revamp their website to better convey their mission of providing equitable healthcare to under-served communities around the globe, on the occassion of their first anniversary. Along with the website, the ask was to create support collaterals on social media and on-ground.

We took up the challenge and not only redesigned the site with a bold, modern design, but also ensured the latest UI and UX best practices that make navigation intuitive and engaging, and most importantly, help showcase important data, right where the user needs it.
