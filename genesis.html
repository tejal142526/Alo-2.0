Challenge

Strengthening awareness of advanced women’s health for Emirati audiences

Burjeel Medical City (BMC) is a world-class tertiary hospital in Abu Dhabi with a strong reputation for advanced care. Yet, awareness of its Genesis Service Line, covering OBGYN, Advanced Gynaecology Institute, Trust Fertility, and Fetal Medicine & Therapy, was limited, particularly among Emirati women.

The challenge was to position BMC as a trusted authority in women’s health, communicate the comprehensiveness of its services, and reach women across all life stages. Additionally, the campaign needed to activate secondary audiences such as male family members and corporate stakeholders, ensuring visibility across Abu Dhabi and the wider UAE.

Approach

A culturally rooted, multi-channel campaign honoring women’s health

To address the challenge, aló designed a campaign that combined strategic insight, creative storytelling, and targeted execution:

1. Research & Insights

Explored Emirati cultural values, life-stage health concerns, and family dynamics to uncover the insight that supporting women strengthens families and society.

2. Creative Strategy

Developed the campaign idea “You Are The Origin”, reframing women’s healthcare as both a medical and cultural imperative. Messaging honored women’s journeys while emphasizing clinical expertise.

3. Digital & Media Strategy

Deployed multi-channel content including reels, static posts, expert videos, and department-specific messaging, with segment-based targeting by age, life stage, and interest.

4. OOH & Broadcast

Large-format visuals in Abu Dhabi and radio scripts reinforced the emotional narrative while highlighting BMC’s medical credibility.

Results

BMC Genesis Service Line achieves stronger visibility and emotional resonance

You Are The Origin” campaign successfully positioned BMC as a trusted authority in women’s health and elevated awareness of its integrated service line

Reinforced BMC’s reputation as a leading destination for women’s healthcare.

Created a culturally resonant narrative that Emirati women recognized and valued.

Provided clear visibility for the Genesis Service Line and its specialized departments.

Strengthened audience understanding of the comprehensive care ecosystem across life stages.

Multi-channel presence increased engagement and made the campaign difficult to overlook within a competitive healthcare landscape

Conclusion

aló’s integrated approach combined cultural insight, creative storytelling, and multi-channel activation to make BMC’s Genesis Service Line visible, relevant, and emotionally resonant. By framing women’s healthcare as both a personal and societal priority, the campaign built lasting awareness, trust, and preference.
